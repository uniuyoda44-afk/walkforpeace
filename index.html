Our Story

There once was a time when practitioners of the Dharma walked barefoot through rice fields and mountain trails — not to reach a destination, but as the practice itself. Walk for Peace was born from that ancient tradition.

We are a nonprofit Buddhist foundation bringing together walkers, monks, communities and donors from across the world in a shared act of compassion.

We always go out as servants. Thong your pact of others. Please register and join to go out.

Your Donation Makes the Walk Possible

Every gift — whether it helps us provide meals for walkers, support temple upkeep, or fund outreach events — turns intention into action. Your generosity keeps this tradition alive and nourishes community connection, compassion, and lasting peace.

Temples Along the Walk

Dhammacetiya Temple

A historic center of peace and meditation, Dhammacetiya Temple welcomes all who seek tranquility and wisdom.

Bodhi Monastery

Known for its ancient Bodhi tree, this monastery is a gathering place for monks and laypeople to practice together.

Lotus Peace Pagoda

A symbol of harmony, the Lotus Peace Pagoda stands as a beacon for non-violence and loving-kindness on the walk route.

Sangha Community Center

A vibrant hub for community service and Dhamma talks, open to all faiths and backgrounds.
